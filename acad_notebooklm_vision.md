# Acad: NotebookLM for Students + Deep Analytics

## Vision Statement

**"NotebookLM lets you chat with your notes. Acad makes your notes work FOR you."**

Acad is an AI-powered academic companion that:
1. **Ingests all your course materials** (like NotebookLM)
2. **Generates personalized study tools** (flashcards, quizzes, summaries)
3. **Tracks your actual learning** (analytics NotebookLM doesn't have)
4. **Adapts to YOUR brain** (spaced repetition, habit patterns, optimal study times)
5. **Predicts your performance** (grade forecasting, weakness detection)

---

## Core Philosophy: Personal AI Study Assistant

### What NotebookLM Does Well (We Keep This)
✅ Upload documents and chat with them
✅ AI-powered Q&A on your content
✅ Source-grounded responses (no hallucinations)
✅ Audio overviews (podcast-style summaries)

### What Acad Adds (NotebookLM Doesn't Have)
🎯 **Learning analytics** - Track mastery over time, not just one-off Q&A
📊 **Performance prediction** - "You're on track for a B+, here's how to get an A"
🧠 **Personalization engine** - Learns YOUR optimal study patterns
📅 **Active study planning** - Tells you what to study today, not just answers questions
🎮 **Motivation systems** - Gamification, streaks, social accountability
🤝 **Collaborative features** - Study with classmates, not alone
⏰ **Spaced repetition** - Remembers what YOU forget
🔮 **Predictive insights** - "You do better on math after 8hrs sleep"
